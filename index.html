About me

I'm Sohani, an HR Manager whose love for people started long before the job title. Growing up endlessly curious about why people do what they do, I naturally gravitated toward psychology and the science of human behavior.

That curiosity is what brought me to HR. I believe understanding what motivates people — what makes them feel belonging, what makes them want to show up — is the single most powerful tool any HR professional can have.

At Uzi World Digital, I've turned that belief into practice: conducting screenings and interviews, coordinating onboarding, supporting HR documentation, and contributing to a culture people actually enjoy being part of.

Key projects & achievements

Initiative 01
Multi-role hiring pipeline
Challenge: Uzi World Digital needed to rapidly scale its team across creative and HR functions with no existing recruitment process. My contribution: I built role-specific JDs, screening rubrics, and structured interview flows for 5+ distinct roles from scratch. Outcome: 15+ candidates screened within weeks, with a consistent evaluation process that reduced guesswork and improved hiring alignment.
15+ screened · 5+ roles filled
Initiative 02
Onboarding experience design
Challenge: New joiners were entering a fast-paced startup with no structured welcome process, leading to early confusion and slower integration. My contribution: Designed a first-week onboarding flow including a welcome checklist, role-clarity document, and culture introduction session. Outcome: New hires reported feeling more prepared and connected, with visibly shorter ramp-up time.
Faster ramp-up · Stronger first impressions
Initiative 03
HR documentation & policy system
Challenge: The company lacked any formal HR documentation — no offer templates, no policy references, no process guides. My contribution: Created and organized the full HR document suite: offer letters, leave policy, conduct guidelines, and an internal HR handbook. Outcome: Built a reliable knowledge base that standardized communication and laid the compliance foundation for team growth.
HR knowledge base built from zero
Initiative 04
Psychology-driven candidate evaluation
Challenge: Interviews were surface-level, focusing only on skills — missing cultural fit and long-term potential. My contribution: Applied behavioral psychology principles to design interview question banks that assess motivation, resilience, and values alignment. Outcome: Hiring decisions became more holistic and intentional, with stronger post-hire alignment reported by team leads.
Deeper, more human hiring decisions
