About me

I'm Sohani, an HR Manager whose love for people started long before the job title. Growing up endlessly curious about why people do what they do, I naturally gravitated toward psychology and the science of human behavior.

That curiosity is what brought me to HR. I believe understanding what motivates people — what makes them feel belonging, what makes them want to show up — is the single most powerful tool any HR professional can have.

At Uzi World Digital, I've turned that belief into practice: conducting screenings and interviews, coordinating onboarding, supporting HR documentation, and contributing to a culture people actually enjoy being part of.
